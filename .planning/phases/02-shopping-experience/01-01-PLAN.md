# Phase 3 Plan: Shopping Experience (Cart & Checkout)

## Backend: Order Management System
- **Migrations**:
  - `create_orders_table`: UUID, (nullable) user_id, total, status, shipping info, payment method.
  - `create_order_items_table`: UUID, order_id, product_variant_id, quantity, price snapshot.
- **Models**:
  - `Order`, `OrderItem` with UUID support and standard relationships.

## Backend: Concurrency & Inventory Control
- **CartService**:
  - Session-based storage for guest cart.
  - CRUD operations for cart items.
- **CheckoutService**:
  - **Transaction Strategy**: Wrap order creation in `DB::transaction()`.
  - **Locking Strategy**: Use `ProductVariant::where('id', $id)->lockForUpdate()->first()` during stock deduction to prevent overselling in high-traffic sessions.
  - **Validation**: Ensure stock exists before locking; re-verify after locking inside transaction.

## Frontend: Nike Kinetic Interaction
- **Slide-over Cart (Live Cart)**:
  - Blade partial for the Offcanvas UI.
  - Alpine.js or vanilla JS to handle open/close and async "Add to Bag" updates.
- **Checkout Flow**:
  - Unified one-page checkout form.
  - Monochromatic "Sumary" panel showing totals and items.
  - Kinetic "Place Order" button.
